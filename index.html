The Selfish Fox and the Smart Stork

A delightful children's story about kindness, sharing and clever friendship

Discover a Forest Adventure Filled with Fun and Valuable Lessons!

The Selfish Fox and the Smart Stork is a heartwarming children's story filled with clever twists, lovable characters, and a timeless moral about kindness and sharing.

When Felix the fox plays a selfish trick on Stella the stork, he learns an important lesson about empathy, fairness, and true friendship. With expressive artwork and a light, engaging narrative style, this story is perfect for young readers and listeners alike.

Parents and teachers love this book for its:

  • Beautiful illustrations that capture children's imagination
  • Gentle approach to teaching important values
  • Relatable characters and engaging storyline
  • Perfect length for bedtime reading or classroom sessions

About the Author

Djibal

Freelance Designer & Storyteller

Djibal is a London-based creator who combines artistic vision with technical expertise to craft beautiful children's stories. With a passion for storytelling and design, Djibal creates books that both entertain and teach valuable life lessons.

"I believe children's books should spark imagination while planting seeds of kindness and empathy. With The Selfish Fox and the Smart Stork, I wanted to create a story that parents would enjoy reading as much as children enjoy hearing."

When not writing and illustrating children's books, Djibal works as a freelance designer bringing creative projects to life for clients around the world.
